## Main

TL;DR: TheoR.net's design is **minimalist, text-first, and information-dense**. Inspirations include [Joe Carlsmith's website](https://joecarlsmith.com/), [Gwern](https://www.gwern.net/) and [Dan Luu](https://danluu.com/)<Footnote id="other-inspiration">My list of favourite personal websites is not limited to those: I endorse Callum McDougall's https://www.perfectlynormal.co.uk/, Slate Star Codex https://slatestarcodex.com/ and others</Footnote>. It is designed to feel snappy with small buttons, sharp edges, minimal animations, no shadows, small gaps and spacings, and contrast colors. It prefers to use OS system UI for fonts, work well on devices of all sizes while maintaining optimal readability.
